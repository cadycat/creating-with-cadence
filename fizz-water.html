Fizzy Water

If you would like to know how to do this awesome experiment, keep on reading

You will need:

  • test tubes
  • baking soda
  • citric acid
  • water
  • (optional)food coloring/color tablets
  • funnel












Step 1:

Fill test tube with water

Step 2(optional):

Put food colring/color tablet in the test tube with water in it and put in baking soda

Step 3:

Use the funnel to put in some citric acid and see what happens

and thats it!
